# Logo Integration Instructions

## Adding Your Logo

1. **Save your logo image** as `qlct_logo.png` in this directory:
   ```
   /Users/alexcs/quantumlogic-chip-tech/static/qlct_logo.png
   ```

2. **Recommended specifications**:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - Size: 200x200 pixels (or any square aspect ratio)
   - Colors: Blue (#667eea) or matching your quantum theme

3. **The logo will appear**:
   - In the Streamlit app sidebar
   - On the FastAPI landing page (/)
   - In the Docker containers when deployed
