## üó£Ô∏è **Delivery Tips**

### **Voice & Tone**
- **Confident but not arrogant**: "We've solved this" not "I think we can"
- **Urgent but not panicked**: "America needs this now" not "We're doomed"
- **Specific not vague**: "32% annual growth" not "really fast growth"
- **Patriotic not political**: "American leadership" not partisan language

### **Body Language**
- Stand tall and maintain eye contact
- Use hand gestures to show scale and movement
- Point to visuals when available
- Lean forward slightly to show engagement
